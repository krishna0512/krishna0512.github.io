Product Description

One of the latest trends in the packaging industry is Bi-axially Oriented Polypropylene Laminated PP Woven bags or commonly refered to as BOPP laminated bags. They have superior photographic appearence and better weather resistance thereby increasing shelf life of the product

BOPP Laminate bags refer to the combination of woven polypropelene used to construct the bags and an additional layer, or laminated surface, of polypropylene i.e. BOPP Film, on which graphich images are printed.
