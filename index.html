About BhashiniDrishti

The Initiative

The initiative harnesses advanced Indian-language AI technologies—including Optical Character Recognition (OCR), Text-to-Speech (TTS), and Automatic Speech Recognition (ASR)—to break long-standing visual and linguistic barriers and enable inclusive digital participation for all.

National-Scale Support

Built on robust national AI models, BhashiniDrishti supports 12 scheduled Indian languages, ensuring seamless access for visually impaired users across India—whether in major cities, small towns, or rural and tribal regions.

Inclusive Digital Transformation

Together, Drishti-Library, Drishti-Sabha, and Drishti-Braille form India’s most comprehensive ecosystem of assistive AI technologies. They bring together speech, language, and tactile intelligence to support:

  • Independent learning
  • Access to governance and public archives
  • Multilingual content consumption
  • Participation in the digital economy

BhashiniDrishti embodies the vision of Accessible India (Sugamya Bharat) and Digital Bharat, ensuring that every person can engage with knowledge in their preferred Indian language.

The Drishti Suite

A unified suite of three flagship applications, each addressing a critical accessibility need.

Turning Every Book into a Voice

Drishti-Library

Converts scanned pages and printed books into clean Unicode text using Bhashini’s state-of-the-art OCR. It then generates natural, human-like audio in multiple Indian languages.

From Pixels to Touch

Drishti-Dot

Transforms scanned documents and images into Grade-1 Braille for 12 Indian languages using AI-driven OCR. Outputs are compatible with Braille displays and embossers.

Voice to Democracy

Drishti-Sabha

India’s first voice-enabled, multilingual question-answering system for legislative debates. Using OCR, ASR, and TTS, the platform digitizes and makes searchable eight decades of proceedings.
